# LinkedIn Video Script: Task 2 Walkthrough

## Opening

Hello everyone, this is my Task 2 submission for the Data Analytics internship: Exploratory Data Analysis and Business Intelligence.

## Dataset Context

For this task, I used the cleaned sales transactions dataset from Task 1. The dataset includes order details, customer segments, regions, sales channels, products, revenue fields, payment modes, and order status.

## EDA Work

I calculated descriptive statistics for numerical fields such as quantity, unit price, discount rate, gross revenue, net revenue, and customer age. I also analyzed categorical fields such as product, region, sales channel, customer segment, and payment mode.

## SQL Business Questions

I wrote SQL queries to answer business questions such as:

- What are the top products by revenue?
- What is the monthly revenue trend?
- Which regions and sales channels perform best?
- Which customer segments contribute the most revenue?
- Which products have the highest return or cancellation rates?
- Which payment modes generate the most completed-order revenue?

## Dashboard Mock-Up

I created a static Excel dashboard mock-up with key KPIs including total revenue, completed orders, average order value, return rate, and cancellation rate. The dashboard also summarizes revenue by month, product, region, sales channel, and customer segment.

## Key Takeaway

This task helped me convert cleaned data into business insights. It also helped me practice SQL, EDA, KPI selection, and dashboard planning for business users.
