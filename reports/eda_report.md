## Executive Summary
The cleaned dataset shows **1,250 orders** and **$5,328,492.40 revenue** across online and retail channels. The business is strongest in **Website**, while **Smart Watch** is the leading product by revenue. Overall gross margin is **20.3%**, and the return rate is **5.2%**.

## Key Insights
- Website and mobile app orders contribute the majority of revenue, showing that the digital buying journey should remain the main growth focus.
- Audio products have a slightly higher return rate than other categories, which suggests a quality expectation or product-fit issue.
- Festive and email-led campaigns lift revenue but should be watched carefully because higher discounts can reduce margin.
- The 25-34 and 35-44 customer segments show strong spending behavior and are good candidates for loyalty campaigns.
